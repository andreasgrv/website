Some facts about myself

My name is Andreas, and I am currently a Masters student digging deeper into Machine Learning and Natural Language Processing at the University of Edinburgh. I was previously working as a research associate at NCSR Demokritos, a member of the Persona group.

My interests are scattered all over the place, but if I had to choose a few labels to stick here - buzzwords included , they would be Machine Learning and Natural Language Processing. If these labels are not familiar, do not despair, I have created my own definitions for them!

  • Machine Learning : A fancier and more pretentious name for pattern recognition in data carried out in order to extract information and rules and see how close to 100% we can get two numbers to be when using these rules on new data.
  • Natural Language Processing : Playing with human input data that represents written text in some human language, and trying to understand why the computer is so bad at analyzing it compared to us.


    • Behind the wishful naming and overstatements however, these areas of Computer Science are extremely interesting and include many fascinating problems one can work upon.

    In my B.Sc. Thesis I worked on word and phrase similarity and tried to align sentences according to word similarities based on word relationships like synonymy, antonymy etc.

    In the year following my graduation I worked on Author Profiling, Named Entity Recognition and Relation Extraction. Our team at NCSR Demokritos placed third in Pan 2015 on the Author Profiling task.
