I am a PhD student at the Institute for Language, Cognition and Computation (ILCC) in Edinburgh, supervised by Adam Lopez and Antonio Vergari. My work focusses on understanding constraints that arise in neural network models and asserting to what extent we can control these constraints and exploit them for our own purposes.

Alongside my PhD, I am also a part-time Research Assistant on Information Extraction from clinical text under the supervision of Beatrice Alex. Here is a demo of our Named Entity Recognition and Negation Detection models for identifying findings (eg. stroke and tumours) from radiology reports.
