Some facts about myself

My name is Andreas, and I am currently a Masters student digging deeper into Machine Learning and Natural Language Processing at the University of Edinburgh. I previously worked as a research associate at NCSR Demokritos, as a member of Persona.

My interests are generally scattered, but during my Masters I have focussed on Natural Language Processing and applied Machine Learning.

More specifically, I have learned a lot about the dark art of training neural networks and have become familiar with the language modelling and dependency parsing literature.

For my M.Sc. dissertation I implemented neural network models for syntactic analysis (Dependency Parsing) of morphologically rich languages.
